Your Local fish and chips shop

Brighton Chippy is located right next to Brighton main train station at the bottom of west hill area. Every day we welcome hundreds of our lovely local people as well as commuters who visit Brighton for work or leisure.
With its high quality and tasty food it is popular with tourists who seeking a lovely traditional fish and chips. Or perhaps some rush in for a quick portion of freshly fried fresh cut chips and maybe even a tasty sausage on the top.
what ever your preference is , there is something for you in Brighton Chippy.
